BeamNG

BeamNG.drive is a realistic vehicle simulation game known for its soft-body physics, which accurately models car damage and handling. Players can explore, crash, and customize vehicles in open-world environments

BeamNG History

BeamNG GmbH was founded in 2012 by a group of physics and simulation enthusiasts. Their goal was to create a realistic driving simulator using soft-body physics. BeamNG.drive launched in 2013 as an early access game, gaining popularity for its detailed vehicle damage, modding support, and open-world driving experience.

BeamNG Fandom

BeamNGs fandom is a passionate community of players, modders, and simulation enthusiasts who celebrate the game’s realistic physics and open-world driving. Fans create custom vehicles, maps, and scenarios, sharing them through forums and mod sites. YouTube and Discord are popular hubs for showcasing crashes, stunts, and experiments. The community values creativity, realism, and technical detail, often pushing the game’s limits with innovative mods and challenges. BeamNG’s open-ended nature fuels endless exploration and fan-driven content.
