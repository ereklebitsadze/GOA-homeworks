About Lelouch

Lelouch vi Britannia, also known as Lelouch Lamperouge, is the brilliant and complex protagonist of the anime Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion. A former prince of the Holy Britannian Empire, he gains a supernatural power called Geass, which allows him to command anyone to obey his orders. Using this power and his strategic genius, he leads a revolutionary group called the Black Knights under the masked identity "Zero" to overthrow Britannia and create a better world.
